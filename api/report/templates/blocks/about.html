About the Midwest Blueprint

The Midwest Conservation Blueprint is a basemap of priority lands and waters for conservation, reflecting a diverse set of 20+ social and environmental values.

It serves as an invitation to better coordinate voluntary conservation actions and investments across the Midwest by:

  1. Focusing and aligning conservation work
  2. Attracting and directing funding for conservation
  3. Connecting partners and opening the door to all audiences
